What Is Link Building?

Why Backlinks Are Still the #1 Ranking Factor

Link building is the process of acquiring hyperlinks from other websites that point to your own. In Google's algorithm, a backlink from a high-authority website acts like a vote of confidence — it tells Google that your content is trustworthy, relevant, and worth ranking highly.

Despite constant algorithm updates, backlinks have remained the single most powerful ranking signal for over two decades. A study of 11.8 million Google search results found that the number of unique domains linking to a page was the #1 factor correlated with higher rankings — more than any on-page element.

However, not all links are created equal. A single backlink from a DA 80 news site can do more for your rankings than a hundred low-quality directory links. This is why Uplyncio focuses exclusively on quality — manually verifying every publisher site for real traffic, genuine authority, and editorial standards.

Our link building marketplace connects businesses with verified publisher sites across 30+ niches. Every placement is transparent — you see the site's DA, DR, monthly traffic, niche, pricing, and sample articles before you order. No surprises, no hidden fees, no black-hat risks.

Whether you need a handful of high-DA links to push a single page to page one, or a full-scale link building campaign to grow your entire domain authority, Uplyncio has the publisher network and the expertise to deliver results that last.
