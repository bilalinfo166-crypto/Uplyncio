FAQ
Questions Answered
Are the backlinks permanent?+
Yes. All guest posts are permanently published on partner sites. We do not remove links after a set period. In the rare case a site goes offline, we replace the link for free.
How long does delivery take?+
Standard delivery is 3–7 business days per post. Expedited delivery (1–3 days) is available for an additional fee. High DA sites (DA70+) may take up to 10 days due to editorial queues.
Do you offer white-label services for agencies?+
Yes. We work with 50+ agencies on a white-label basis. Unbranded reports, private dashboard, and a dedicated account manager. Contact us for agency pricing and volume discounts.
Do you write the content or do I provide it?+
Both options are available. All packages include content writing at no extra cost. If you prefer to supply your own article, we'll review it for SEO and publisher guidelines before submission.
What if I'm not satisfied with a placement?+
If a post doesn't meet the agreed site metrics or quality standards, we'll replace it with a comparable site at no cost. Client satisfaction is guaranteed on every order.
Which payment methods do you accept?+
We accept PayPal, Wise, bank transfer, and cryptocurrency (USDT/BTC). Payment is due before work begins. Bulk orders (10+ posts) can be invoiced with net-7 terms.
