⚠️ Violating these rules may result in order cancellation, rating deduction, or account suspension.
01 Accept only orders you can complete within the required timeframe (3–7 days). Do not ignore orders as auto-rejection damages your completion rate.

📋 Withdrawal Terms & Conditions
1. Publishers can submit a maximum of 2 withdrawal requests per month — one per window. Requests outside the designated window dates will not be processed and must be resubmitted in the next available cycle.
2. Window 1: Submit request between 20th–25th of the month. Payment will be sent to your account between 1st–5th of the following month.
3. Window 2: Submit request between 10th–15th of the month. Payment will be sent to your account between 20th–25th of the same month.
4. The minimum withdrawal amount is $60 USD. Requests below this threshold will be rejected. Your balance will remain intact until it reaches the minimum.
5. Platform service commission of 7.9% for PayPal and 9% for USDT will be deducted from your withdrawal amount. We only support PayPal and USDT as payment methods. The final amount received will reflect these deductions.
6. You are responsible for providing correct and valid payment details (PayPal email or USDT wallet address). Uplyncio is not liable for funds sent to incorrect addresses provided by the publisher.
7. Only earnings from fully completed and approved tasks are eligible for withdrawal. Reserved balance (tasks in progress) cannot be withdrawn until the client approves the order.
8. Uplyncio reserves the right to delay or withhold payment if there is an active dispute, quality complaint, or suspected violation of publisher guidelines on your account.
9. By submitting a withdrawal request, you confirm that all published content on your site meets Uplyncio's quality standards and that no client has raised an unresolved dispute against your account.
10. For any payment-related issues, contact us at info@uplyncio.com. Include your Task IDs and withdrawal request date for faster resolution.
