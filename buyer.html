1. Add Funds

Click your profile (top right) → Add Funds. We accept PayPal, USDT TRC20, Wise, and Bank Transfer. Minimum top-up is $20. Balance appears instantly (crypto) or within a few hours (bank).

2. Browse & Filter Sites

Use the filter bar or left sidebar to narrow down by:

  • Niche — pick your industry
  • Price — set your budget range
  • Country / Language — geo targeting
3. Place an Order
  • Click Buy Post on any site
  • Enter your Target URL + Anchor Text
  • Provide article or let publisher write it

  • 12-month link guarantee — if removed, we replace it free
Frequently Asked Questions
Are the backlinks dofollow? +
Yes — all Uplyncio links are dofollow by default. You can also filter the marketplace to show only dofollow sites.
How long does delivery take? +
Publishers have 3 days to accept, then 3–10 days to publish. Most deliver within 5 days. Each site's TAT (turnaround time) is shown on its listing card.
What if I'm not satisfied with delivery? +
Use the order chat to request a revision before confirming delivery. Payment is held in escrow — publisher only gets paid when you approve. If publisher cancels or fails to deliver, your balance is refunded in full.
What is Campaign Mode? +
Campaign Mode lets you set your link building goals once (niche, budget, DA/DR minimum, anchor texts) and the system finds matching sites and places orders automatically — instead of ordering one by one. Access it from the sidebar → 📊 Campaigns.
Need help? Contact us +
Email us at info@uplyncio.com — we respond within 24 hours. You can also use the 💬 Ace chat button (bottom right) for instant answers.
