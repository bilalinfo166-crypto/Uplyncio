Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Learn how Uplyncio collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Uplyncio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our platform at uplyncio.com. By using Uplyncio, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Registration data: Name, email address, password (encrypted), and account role (Buyer or Publisher)
  • Profile data: Profile picture, phone number, billing address, website URL, payment method details
  • Verification data: Email verification codes, two-factor authentication settings

1.2 Transaction Data

  • Order details including target URLs, anchor texts, keywords, and special instructions
  • Payment history, wallet balances, withdrawal requests, and invoice records
  • Publisher site listings including DA, DR, traffic, pricing, and categories

1.3 Usage Data

  • Browser type, IP address, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on platform, and click behavior
  • Search queries and filter preferences on the marketplace

1.4 Communications

Messages sent through the Uplyncio chat system, support emails, and notification preferences are stored to facilitate orders and resolve disputes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: Matching Buyers with Publishers, processing orders, and facilitating payments
  • Account management: Verifying your identity, managing your account settings, and maintaining security
  • Communication: Sending order notifications, account alerts, and platform updates
  • Platform improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity, abuse, and Terms violations
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Between Users

When a Buyer places an order, limited information (target URL, anchor text, article content, and order instructions) is shared with the assigned Publisher to complete the order. Publisher site information is publicly visible on the marketplace to all Buyers.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform, including payment processors, email delivery services, and analytics tools. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority. We will notify you of such requests to the extent permitted by law.

3.4 We Never Sell Your Data

Uplyncio does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We implement access controls, regular security audits, and secure communication protocols (HTTPS) to protect your information.

While we take every reasonable measure to protect your data, no system is completely immune to security breaches. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you promptly.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Uplyncio uses browser local storage to maintain your session, save preferences, and store your marketplace data locally for performance. We use cookies for authentication and analytics purposes. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via your account settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to specific uses of your data, including marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@uplyncio.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records are retained for 7 years for legal and financial compliance. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Uplyncio is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has registered, we will promptly delete their account and associated data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

  • Email: info@uplyncio.com
  • Response time: Within 2 business days

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.
