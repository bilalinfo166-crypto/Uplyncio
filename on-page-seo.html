⚙️ On-Page SEO

Fix What Google Can See & Watch Rankings Climb

On-page SEO is the foundation that determines whether Google understands what your pages are about and whether they deserve to rank. We conduct deep technical audits, optimize every content element, and implement structured data that gives your site a decisive edge over competitors.

Get Started → View Pricing

Deep Technical Site Audit

Before optimizing anything, we audit your entire site to identify every technical issue holding your rankings back. Crawl errors, broken links, duplicate content, thin pages, slow load times — we find everything and prioritize fixes by ranking impact.

Our audits go beyond surface-level Screaming Frog scans. We analyze Core Web Vitals, internal link equity distribution, JavaScript rendering issues, and indexation problems that most SEOs completely miss.

  • Complete crawl and indexation analysis
  • Core Web Vitals assessment and fixes
  • Duplicate content and cannibalization detection
  • Internal link structure optimization

Content Optimization & Keyword Mapping

Great content that is poorly optimized will never rank as well as it should. We analyze your target keywords, map them to the right pages, optimize title tags and meta descriptions, improve content depth, and ensure proper heading structure throughout.

We do not stuff keywords — we use semantic SEO principles to help Google understand the full topical context of each page, which drives rankings for dozens of related terms beyond just the primary keyword.

  • Title tag and meta description optimization
  • Heading structure and content hierarchy fixes
  • Semantic keyword and entity optimization
  • Content gap analysis and expansion

Schema Markup & Structured Data

Structured data helps Google understand your content faster and can unlock rich results — star ratings, FAQs, breadcrumbs, and more — that dramatically increase your click-through rate even before your rankings improve.

We implement the right schema types for your business and validate everything in Google's Rich Results Test. Properly implemented schema is one of the highest-ROI technical SEO investments available.

  • Organization, Product, and Article schema
  • FAQ and HowTo markup for rich results
  • Local Business schema for local visibility
  • Breadcrumb and Sitelinks schema implementation
