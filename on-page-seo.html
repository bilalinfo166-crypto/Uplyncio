What We Do

Every Element, Perfectly Optimized

🏷️

Title Tags & Meta Descriptions

We write compelling, keyword-rich title tags under 60 characters and meta descriptions under 155 characters that improve click-through rates and clearly signal your page topic to Google's crawlers.

📋

Header Tag Hierarchy (H1-H4)

Proper heading structure is both an SEO signal and a usability factor. We organize your H1, H2, H3, and H4 tags to establish clear content hierarchy and target long-tail keyword variations naturally.

🔑

Keyword Optimization & Density

Strategic placement of primary and secondary keywords in body content, first paragraph, image alt texts, and URL slugs — all without crossing into over-optimization territory that Google penalizes.

🔗

Internal Linking Strategy

A well-designed internal link structure distributes page authority across your site and helps Google discover all your content. We map and implement internal links that keep users engaged and boost rankings site-wide.

📊

Schema Markup & Rich Snippets

We implement JSON-LD structured data for Articles, FAQs, Products, LocalBusiness, Reviews, and more — dramatically increasing your chances of appearing in Google's rich results and featured snippets.

🖼️

Image SEO & Alt Optimization

Every image is renamed with descriptive filenames, given keyword-rich alt attributes, compressed for speed, and converted to WebP format for better Core Web Vitals scores and image search visibility.
