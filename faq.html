Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Uplyncio.

For Buyers

How does Uplyncio work?

Browse our marketplace of 20,000+ verified publisher sites. Filter by DA, niche, country, and price. Place your order, provide your content (or let the publisher write it), and your link goes live within 3–10 business days.

Are the links dofollow?

Yes. All links on Uplyncio are guaranteed dofollow unless explicitly stated otherwise in the site listing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, USDT (TRC20), Wise, and Bank Transfer. Add funds to your Uplyncio balance and use it to place orders.

What if my order is rejected?

If a publisher rejects your order, your funds are immediately returned to your balance. You can then order from another site.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No minimum. You can place a single order starting from $30.

For Publishers

How do I join as a publisher?

Click "Become a Publisher", create your account, and add your website(s). Set your prices, requirements, and start receiving orders.

How much can I earn?

Publishers typically earn $30–$2,000+ per post depending on their site's authority and niche. You set your own price.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed via PayPal (7.5% fee) or USDT TRC20 (9.5% fee). Withdrawals are processed within 3–5 business days.

Can I reject orders I don't like?

Yes. You have 3 business days to accept or reject any order. You have full editorial control.
